Are you tired of the mundane? Does your soul yearn for pronouncements that defy logic, tickle the absurd, and perhaps, just perhaps, offer profound insights wrapped in a riddle? Then you've stumbled into the right corner of the digital cosmos, my friend!

Born from the digital ether and fueled by the whispers of forgotten limericks and half-remembered dreams, this magnificent contraption conjures quotes that will make you ponder, chuckle, or simply stare blankly at your screen in delightful confusion.

- **Quirky Quintessences:** Expect the unexpected. No trite platitudes here.
- **Grammatical Gymnastics:** Sometimes it sticks to the rules, sometimes it performs linguistic acrobatics. It's all part of the charm!

So, step forth, brave seeker of the strange. Let `quotes-gen` whisper its digital secrets into your waiting ears. Just don't ask it about the meaning of life... unless you're prepared for an answer involving rubber chickens and the price of tea in ####.
